Technica is calling on students across UMD to lead our 2021 organizing team!

As a Technica director, you’ll have the unique opportunity to lead one of the largest and most impactful technology nonprofits in the country, work with some of the kindest and most brilliant people at UMD, and promote our message of inclusion in STEM for the largest all-women and non-binary hackathon in the world!

What do Directors do?

  • Manage everything related to a specific area of the event (e.g. marketing, design, operations)
  • Lead their own team of 4 - 10 organizers
  • Craft the overall vision and strategy of the event with the co-executive directors

What Positions Are Available?

  • Technology Co-Director
  • Videography/Photography Director
  • Operations Co-Director
Application Deadline: February 19th @ 11:59PM EST
