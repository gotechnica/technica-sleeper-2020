Our Story

Technica was founded in 2015 by empowered students at the University of Maryland who recognized the need for an inclusive all-women & non-binary space in the hackathon scene.

As the world’s largest all-women & non-binary hackathon, we are often asked how one can support our cause. With time constraints and distance in mind, we’ve created a way for individuals to offer support: financial donations.

We know money can be tight, so any and all donations are greatly appreciated. Pitching in a few bucks towards our cause means we can continue offering the highest quality experience for our hackers.

Our Donor Promise

We promise to respect your donation by only using donor funds to support our mission of reaching gender equity in tech. The most high cost ways we will spend funds this year are mailing supplies to hackers, providing supplies for mini activities all weekend long, and ensuring the virtual Technica experience is still amazing through our custom virtual platform.

Additionally, Technica is a 501(c)(3) public charity, so your donations are tax-deductible.
